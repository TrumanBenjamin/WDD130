Types of Bananas

We're all familiar with the common, everyday, yellow banana. This is what most of us see in our local stores, and what most of us eat. However, there are many more types of bananas than you might know. One of my favorites is the red banana or 'Red Dacca.' This banana is sweeter and softer than the average yellow banana. They are perfect for adding a little more banana flavor to any of your breakfast items!

The second type of banana that I want to highlight is the Burro Banana, or 'Musa paradisiaca'. This banana is a slightly different shape than other bananas, and is known for its slightly lemon-y flavor! These bananas are grown primarily in Mexico. They stand out from most other bananas because of their triangular shape, as you can see from the picture. These bananas are also a seeded variety of banana, so be careful not to bite down too hard and break a tooth!
