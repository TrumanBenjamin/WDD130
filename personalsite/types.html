Types of Bananas

We're all familiar with the common, everyday, yellow banana. This is what most of us see in our local stores, and what most of us eat. However, there are many more types of bananas than you might know. One of my favorites is the red banana or 'Red Dacca.'

The second type of banana that I want to highlight is the Burro Banana, or 'Musa paradisiaca'. This banana is a slightly different shape than other bananas, and is known for its slightly lemon-y flavor! These bananas are grown primarily in Mexico.
